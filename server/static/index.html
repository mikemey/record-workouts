Workout Records is an easy way to control and display your workouts for your standard iOS Health App. 315px width [screenshot main screen] You can choose how you would like to save your workouts in your Health App: 1. As a combined workout record with the distance covered and active energy burned 2. As individual records for both the distance covered and active energy burned 3.
