Workout Records

Workout Records is an easy way to control and display your workouts for your standard iOS Health App.

You can choose between 3 different ways how your workout is stored in your iOS Health App:

  • Combined workout record

    Create an activity with a single workout record including the distance covered and active energy burned.

  • Individual workout records

    Create a workout activity with separate records for the distance covered and active energy burned

  • Energy only record

    Create a single record for your active energy burned without a specific activity type

Get Started

To allow Workout Records to save and read your health records, select either "Turn All Categories On" or choose the specific types in your iOS Health App:

Close the Health screen by tapping "Allow".

You can change the permissions anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy > Health.
